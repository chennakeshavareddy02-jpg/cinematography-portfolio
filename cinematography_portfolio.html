About

I'm Chennakeshava, a cinematographer and visual storyteller based in Bengaluru. My work focuses on documentary filmmaking that captures authentic human experiences and social narratives with visual precision and narrative care.

As a Media & Journalism student at Azim Premji University, I've developed a deep understanding of visual language, composition, and the power of cinema to illuminate social issues. My cinematography is grounded in careful observation, thoughtful framing, and a commitment to portraying subjects with dignity and nuance.

Through films like Jyothi and The Space We Borrow, I explore how visual storytelling can make meaning—documenting the overlooked, centering the marginalized, and creating space for their voices.
